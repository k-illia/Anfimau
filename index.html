Project history
Our CEO and founder Mikhail Anfimau has over 20 years of experience in IT: 2002-2012 he worked for various Software Vendors as part of outstaffed teams. In 2013, after a brief career pause to finish his Master’s studies in Norway in Industrial Logistics field, he moved to Germany to lead a massive project for Philip Morris International with overall team size of up to 40 developers and QA engineers. Being massively adopted and used by thousands of employees in regional offices in 80 countries, the project was of a great success. After reaching high maturity, the project was handed over. In 2019, after a year in a role of Senior Infrastructure Architect at Deutsche Lufthansa AG in the central IT Department, Mikhail started his solo consulting and engineering career. While successfully performing several projects for such clients like Vattenfall Energy Trading, Carl Zeiss Digital Innovation and GEA, there arose a need to scale out the services by assigning more engineers to perform specific tasks. That way, our company was founded in 2021, quickly acquiring a talented team of 30 engineers, both contractors and employees, in several countries like Poland, Ukraine, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, Georgia and Uzbekistan. We perfectly work together despite the distance and time zones, delivering our best services and knowledge to our clients.
